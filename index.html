ACM-W BPHC

ACM-W advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the contributions of technical women. ACM-W warmly welcomes the participation of all individuals who are interested in advancing ACM’s mission and core values.
